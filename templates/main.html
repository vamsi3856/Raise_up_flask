About Us

We want to help women speak out about problems that they face and help them find solutions for issues they encounter. We will help them to get virtual consulation about different issues that they face such as sexual harassment, domestic abuse, or gender discrimination. We provide consultation to help women know that the negative treatments they faced were not their faults and help them speak up to seek help. We have different forums for women to connect with others who face same issues to share and seek advice.
